FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the best diet plan for weight loss?
There is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. The best diet plan for weight loss varies from person to person based on their age, gender, weight, height, and activity level. A registered dietitian like Shreya Katyal can help you create a personalized diet plan based on your individual needs.
How much weight can I expect to lose in a week?
The amount of weight you can expect to lose in a week depends on various factors such as your starting weight, diet plan, exercise routine, and metabolism. However, a safe and healthy weight loss is between 0.5 to 1 kilogram per week.
How do I stay motivated to stick to my weight loss diet?
Staying motivated can be challenging while following a weight loss diet. Set realistic goals, track your progress, reward yourself, and surround yourself with a supportive environment to stay motivated.
Can I have cheat meals while following a weight loss diet?
Yes, you can have cheat meals while following a weight loss diet. However, limit it to once a week and watch your portion size. Enjoy your favorite foods in moderation to avoid derailing your weight loss journey.
Are weight loss supplements effective?
Weight loss supplements are not a magic pill for weight loss. Some supplements can have side effects and may not be safe for everyone. It is important to consult a healthcare professional before taking any weight loss supplements.
